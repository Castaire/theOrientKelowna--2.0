[  2020-05-30 : Saturday : UPDATE 01  ]

After some serious thought on how we'll be proceeding in the new Phase 2, we have decided to remain offering take-out service only.

As you may know, our dining area is pretty small and with the necessary social distancing measures in place, we would end up being only able to open 2-3 tables which currently isn't very practical!

That said, we will be revising our response as the pandemic and BC health regulations change.
We would like to thank you again for your continued support and are already looking forward to posting new updates here soon!
